About Campus Chronicles

Campus Chronicles is an initiative dedicated to preserving and sharing the vibrant culture, memories, and stories of K.R. Mangalam University. Our goal is to build an engaging digital platform that connects students, alumni, and visitors through a shared sense of heritage.

From interactive college maps to an immersive memory wall where students can upload and explore moments from fests, events, and everyday campus life, our project aims to create a virtual archive that grows with every contribution — inspiring belongingness and nostalgia across generations.
